# Voice Profile: Default

## Overview

**Name:** Default
**Type:** Template
**Created:** 2026-02-04
**Description:** Baseline voice profile for new projects

## Characteristics

### Sentence Structure
- **Average length:** 15-25 words
- **Variation:** Moderate (mix short punchy with longer flowing)
- **Complexity:** Accessible, clear syntax

### Word Choice
- **Register:** Literary but not pretentious
- **Specificity:** Prefer concrete nouns and active verbs
- **Vocabulary:** Broad but natural

### Tone
- **Emotional temperature:** Warm, engaged
- **Narrative distance:** Close (intimate access to POV thoughts)
- **Formality:** Conversational, natural

### Imagery
- **Sensory emphasis:** Visual primary, tactile secondary
- **Metaphor frequency:** Occasional, organic
- **Style:** Grounded in physical reality

### Rhythm
- **Pacing:** Matches scene energy
- **Paragraph length:** Varied (2-6 sentences typical)
- **Dialogue:** Natural, subtext-rich
